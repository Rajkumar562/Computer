WELCOME TO RAJDHANI COLLEGE

Rajdhani College was established in 1964 by the Delhi Administration under the name of Government College and was renamed as Rajdhani College when the Delhi administration vested its governance in an autonomous Governing Body.

Rajdhani College is a constituent college of the University of Delhi and has completed 50 glorious years of academic excellence.
